Who Are We?

The Strategy & Consulting Club of IIM Rohtak

We are a student-run organization with the goal of giving students a forum to learn more about the consulting industry. We work in partnership with faculty and corporates to scale up consulting culture on campus, bringing interesting live projects and publications, giving students an edge to think strategically in their domains.

"The 'W' in Wazir does not stand for work, but stands for 'WE', because we are not a club, we are a family."

V

Vision

Foster a culture of knowledge and engagement that reaches across IIM Rohtak and inspires minds beyond.

M

Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and network necessary to excel in the consulting industry.
